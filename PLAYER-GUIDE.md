# 🎮 Player Guide - Super Easy Mode!

## Hi! Welcome to Aura's Adventure! 👋

This game is designed to be **super easy and relaxing** for you to play while your partner helps with the tricky science questions. Don't worry - you can't really lose!

---

## 🕹️ Controls (Very Simple!)

### Keyboard Controls:
- **W** or **↑ (Up Arrow)** = Jump
- **A** or **← (Left Arrow)** = Move Left  
- **D** or **→ (Right Arrow)** = Move Right
- **S** or **↓ (Down Arrow)** = (Not really needed, but moves down)
- **Space Bar** = Jump (alternative)
- **ESC** = Pause the game

### That's It! Just 3 buttons really:
1. **Left** to go back
2. **Right** to go forward
3. **Jump** to go up

---

## 🎯 What to Do

### Your Goal:
**Just move from left to right and collect shiny things!**

1. **Move Right** → Keep going right to reach the end
2. **Collect Items** → Run into colorful circles and stars
3. **Grab Power-Ups** → Touch floating orbs for cool abilities
4. **Avoid Enemies** → Orange/red squares (they just push you back, no damage!)
5. **Reach Gates** → When you hit a colored wall, your partner solves a puzzle
6. **Get to the End** → Keep going right until you win!

---

## ✨ What You'll See

### Good Things (Collect These!):
- 🪙 **Yellow Circles** = Coins (15 points)
- ⭐ **Yellow Stars** = Stars (30 points)
- 💎 **Purple Hexagons** = Crystals (50 points)
- 🔵 **Blue Orbs** = Speed boost (move faster!)
- 🟢 **Green Orbs** = Double jump (jump twice!)
- 🟣 **Purple Orbs** = Invincibility (can't be touched!)
- 🟠 **Orange Orbs** = Shield (extra protection!)

### Things to Avoid (Not Scary!):
- 🟧 **Orange Squares** = Slow enemies (just push you back)
- 🟥 **Red Squares** = (There are none in easy mode!)
- 🪨 **Gray Blocks** = Obstacles (jump over them)

### Your Character:
- 💗 **Pink Blob** = That's you! (Aura)
- ❤️❤️❤️❤️❤️ **5 Hearts** = Your health (you have lots!)

---

## 🌟 Special Abilities

### You Can Jump TWICE!
1. Press **Jump** once = First jump
2. Press **Jump** again while in the air = Second jump!
3. This makes it super easy to reach high places

### You Move Fast!
- Your character moves quickly
- Easy to dodge enemies
- Fun to zoom around collecting things

### You're Tough!
- You have **5 hearts** (lots of health!)
- Enemies **don't hurt you** (just push you back)
- You basically can't die!

---

## 🎨 Tips for Fun

### 1. **Collect Everything!**
- Try to grab all the coins, stars, and crystals
- Build up combos by collecting quickly
- Watch your score go up!

### 2. **Explore!**
- Jump around and explore the level
- Look for hidden crystals (worth 50 points!)
- Take your time, no rush!

### 3. **Use Power-Ups!**
- Speed boost = Zoom around super fast
- Double jump = Reach high places
- Invincibility = Run through enemies
- Shield = Extra protection

### 4. **Don't Worry About Enemies!**
- They're slow and easy to avoid
- They don't hurt you (just push you back)
- There are only 1-2 per level
- Just jump over them!

### 5. **Have Fun!**
- Listen to the cute sound effects
- Watch the sparkly particle effects
- Enjoy the colorful world
- Relax and play at your own pace

---

## 🚪 When You Reach a Gate

### What Happens:
1. You'll run into a **colored wall** (gate)
2. The game **pauses** automatically
3. A **puzzle appears** on screen
4. Your partner **solves the puzzle**
5. Gate **opens** and you continue!

### Your Job:
- Just wait and watch
- Cheer your partner on!
- Maybe help think through the answer
- Celebrate when they solve it!

## 💕 Playing Together

### Perfect Team Activity:
- **You**: Focus on the fun gameplay
- **Partner**: Focus on the brain puzzles
- **Together**: Beat the levels!

### Communication:
- Call out when you reach a gate
- Share excitement when collecting items
- Celebrate puzzle solutions together
- Have fun and laugh!

## 🎵 Sounds & Effects

### Listen For:
- 🎶 **Ding!** = Collected an item
- 🎵 **Boing!** = Jumped
- ✨ **Sparkle!** = Got a power-up
- 🎺 **Fanfare!** = Solved a puzzle
- 🎉 **Victory!** = Completed the level

### Watch For:
- ✨ Sparkles when you collect things
- 💫 Trails when you move
- 🎆 Bursts when you get power-ups
- 🌟 Glowing effects everywhere!

---

## 🆘 If You Get Stuck

### No Problem!
- **Press ESC** to pause
- **Take a break** if needed
- **Ask for help** from your partner
- **Restart the level** if you want (no penalty!)

### Remember:
- You can't really lose
- Enemies don't hurt you
- You have 5 hearts
- Take all the time you need
- It's meant to be relaxing!

---

## 🏆 Winning

### How to Complete a Level:
1. Keep moving right
2. Collect items along the way
3. Let your partner solve gate puzzles
4. Reach the far right side
5. **Victory!** 🎉

### What Happens Next:
- See your score
- Unlock the next level
- Choose to continue or take a break
- Your progress saves automatically!
