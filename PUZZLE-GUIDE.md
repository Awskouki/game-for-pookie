## 📐 Mathematics Puzzles

### Topics Covered:

#### Calculus
- **Integration**: ∫(2x + 3)dx = ?
- **Derivatives**: d/dx(sin x) = ?
- **Limits**: lim(x→0) (sin x)/x = ?

#### Advanced Algebra
- **Euler's Identity**: e^(iπ) + 1 = ?
- **Logarithms**: log₁₀(100) = ?
- **Quadratic Formula**: x = (-b ± √(b²-4ac))/2a

#### Geometry
- **Pythagorean Theorem**: a² + b² = c²
- **Triangle Angles**: Sum = 180°

### Example Questions:
```
Q: Solve: ∫(2x + 3)dx = ?
A: x² + 3x + C

Q: What is the derivative of sin(x)?
A: cos(x)

Q: What is e^(iπ) + 1 = ?
A: 0 (Euler's Identity)
```

## 🧪 Chemistry Puzzles

### Topics Covered:

#### Fundamental Constants
- **Avogadro's Number**: 6.022 × 10²³
- **pH Scale**: Neutral = 7

#### Atomic Structure
- **Electron Configuration**: 1s² 2s² 2p²
- **Hybridization**: sp, sp², sp³

#### Chemical Equations
- **Balancing**: C₃H₈ + 5O₂ → 3CO₂ + 4H₂O
- **Ideal Gas Law**: PV = nRT

#### Advanced Concepts
- **Molecular Geometry**: Tetrahedral, linear, etc.
- **Oxidation States**: Calculating charges
- **Gibbs Free Energy**: ΔG < 0 = spontaneous
- **Rate Laws**: rate = k[A] for first-order

### Example Questions:
```
Q: What is Avogadro's number?
A: 6.022 × 10²³

Q: Balance: C₃H₈ + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O
A: C₃H₈ + 5O₂ → 3CO₂ + 4H₂O

Q: What is the electron configuration of Carbon (Z=6)?
A: 1s² 2s² 2p²
```

## ⚛️ Physics Puzzles

### Topics Covered:

#### Classical Physics
- **Newton's Laws**: F = ma
- **Universal Gravitation**: F = G(m₁m₂)/r²
- **Coulomb's Law**: F = k(q₁q₂)/r²

#### Modern Physics
- **Einstein's E = mc²**: Mass-energy equivalence
- **Speed of Light**: c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
- **Planck's Constant**: h = 6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s

#### Quantum Mechanics
- **Heisenberg Uncertainty**: Δx·Δp ≥ ℏ/2
- **Schrödinger Equation**: Ĥψ = Eψ
- **de Broglie Wavelength**: λ = h/p

#### Thermodynamics
- **First Law**: ΔU = Q - W
- **Maxwell's Equations**: ∇·E = ρ/ε₀

### Example Questions:
```
Q: What is Einstein's mass-energy equivalence?
A: E = mc²

Q: What is the Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle?
A: Δx·Δp ≥ ℏ/2

Q: What is Planck's constant?
A: 6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s
```
