# 🚪 ESCAPE ROOM UPDATE

## Cleaner, Darker, More Focused!

The game has been updated to be more like an **escape room** with a **minimalist horror aesthetic**.

---

## ✅ **What Was Removed**

### **Power-Ups** (Completely Removed)
- ❌ No more speed boosts
- ❌ No more shields
- ❌ No more invincibility
- ❌ No more double jump power-ups
- ❌ No more colorful floating orbs

**Why?** Too many colors cluttered the screen. The dark horror theme works better with minimal visual elements.

### **Collectible Variety** (Simplified)
- ❌ No more coins (yellow)
- ❌ No more stars (golden)
- ✅ **Only dark red crystals (souls)**

**Why?** One collectible type = cleaner aesthetic, more focused gameplay.

---

## 🎨 **Visual Changes**

### **Collectibles**
- **Before**: 30-100+ colorful items (coins, stars, crystals)
- **Now**: 8-20 dark red soul crystals with red glow
- **Appearance**: Dark red hexagonal crystals with pulsing red aura
- **Value**: 100 points each (higher value, fewer items)

### **Enemies**
- **Before**: 1-2 enemies per level
- **Now**: 0-2 enemies (mostly empty rooms)
- **Behavior**: Very slow patrol (0.3-0.6 speed)
- **Purpose**: Atmosphere, not challenge

### **Obstacles**
- **Before**: Few scattered obstacles
- **Now**: Room-like structures with walls and barriers
- **Style**: Dark stone (#1c1917) walls and platforms
- **Layout**: Creates escape room feel with corridors

---

- ✅ **Move and jump** (that's it!)
- ✅ **Collect souls** (dark crystals)

### **No Power-Up Management**
- ❌ No need to track power-up timers

### **Cleaner HUD**

## 🎯 **Escape Room Feel**

### **What Makes It Feel Like an Escape Room**

1. **Structured Spaces**
   - Walls create defined rooms
   - Corridors connect areas
   - Gates block progress

2. **Puzzle-Focused**
   - Main challenge is solving puzzles
   - Navigation is secondary
   - No combat or power-ups

3. **Atmospheric**
   - Empty, eerie rooms
   - Minimal enemies (just atmosphere)
   - Dark, ominous lighting

4. **Linear Progression**
   - Move through rooms
   - Solve puzzle at each gate
   - Progress to next area
   - Escape at the end

5. **Tension Without Action**
   - No frantic gameplay
   - Slow, methodical exploration
   - Brain-focused challenges
   - Escape room pacing
